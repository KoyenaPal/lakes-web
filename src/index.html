Why Model Lakes?

Data lakes have revolutionized how raw, diverse, and large-scale data is stored and accessed. Instead of forcing data into rigid structures, data lakes allow flexible discovery, annotation, and version management — enabling users to extract insights without predefined schemas. Similarly, model lakes — a system containing numerous heterogeneous pre-trained models and related data in their natural formats - provide a structured way to navigate the vast and complex landscape of AI models, making them easier to search, analyze, and utilize effectively.
